Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about ToVector.ai

General Questions

What is ToVector.ai?

ToVector.ai is a powerful SaaS platform that converts your images into high-quality vector graphics using advanced AI technology.

How accurate is the vectorization?

Our AI-powered vectorization provides highly accurate results, especially for logos, icons, and illustrations. You can preview the results before using your credits.

What file formats do you support?

We support input formats including PNG, JPG, JPEG, and BMP. Vector outputs are available in SVG, AI, and EPS formats.

Credits & Pricing

How do credits work?

Credits are used to vectorize images. One credit typically converts one image, with preview options available before using credits.

Do credits expire?

No, your credits never expire. You can use them whenever you need them.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, unused credits can be refunded within 30 days of purchase. Contact our support team for assistance.

Technical Questions

What's the maximum file size?

We accept images up to 50MB in size. For larger files, please contact our support team.

How long does vectorization take?

Most images are processed within seconds. Complex images may take up to a minute.

Can I batch process images?

Yes, you can upload and process multiple images at once. Each image will use one credit.
